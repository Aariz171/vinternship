When I started this internship at VLED labs, I felt depressed as I was completely new to the MERN Stack. I felt that how am I able to continue this internship. But my views are changed as I continue with the internship.

The very first of this internship is to complete the self-paced course through the ViBe portal. The experience in this ViBe portal is very unique. Unlike other online courses and internships, this ViBe portal uses a very unique model of proctoring through camera and microphone, which helps in maintaining discipline and consistency in studies. 

After the completion of course in the ViBe portal, we move forward in the case study section which consists of multiple mini projects on different topics. The case studies helps us to move forward from my comfort zone and think like the developers, that how to tackle different real life problems that will come to us. Through this internship I have learned a lot of things about MERN Stack that includes java script, typescript, Mongo Db, React and Express.

 The Breakout discussions and live sessions helps to resolve the doubts and learn new things along with peers in this virtual environment. These live sessions and Discord servers helps to connect to the peers, discuss doubts, share knowledge. The internship felt like a real offline internship rather than virtual internship due to these interactive environment.

A heartfelt thanks to Prof. Sudarshan Iyengar Sir for his mentorship. Also I want to extend my gratitude to VLED labs, ANNAM.AI, Indian Institute of Technology, Ropar, NPTEL. Special thanks to Indian Institute of Technology, Madras through which I got this wonderful opportunity.
